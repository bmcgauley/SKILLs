## Text Positioning — Image Assets

### Main Headline
- Position: Center frame, lower third
- Size: Large (dominant visual element)
- Color: White #FFFFFF
- Typeface: Space Mono Bold, ALL CAPS

### Subheadline
- Position: Directly below Main Headline, tight spacing
- Size: Medium (clearly subordinate to headline)
- Color: Cyan #00FFFF
- Typeface: Space Mono Regular, Title Case

### CTA
- Position: Bottom-right corner, small
- Size: Small (supporting, not competing)
- Color: Magenta #FF0080
- Typeface: Space Mono Regular
- Format: "Action → destination" (e.g., "Watch Now → mcgauleylabs.news")

### Category Badge
- Position: Top-left corner
- Style: Text in brackets, e.g., [MACRO ECONOMICS]
- Color: Magenta #FF0080
- Typeface: Space Mono Bold, ALL CAPS, small

### Footer Bar (slides/infographics)
- Position: Bottom edge, full width
- Left: Date range (e.g., "DATE: FEB 15–FEB 22, 2026")
- Right: "IMAGINARII | SOURCE: MCGAULEYLABS.NEWS"
- Typeface: Space Mono Regular, small, white at 60% opacity

## Visual Language & Mood

**Overall aesthetic:** Dark tech / data terminal / cinematic. NOT corporate. NOT stock photo.

**Visual motifs welcome:**
- Wireframe globe or map elements in cyan
- Circuit trace patterns, grid overlays
- Data dashboard aesthetic
- Glow effects in cyan; hot spot accents in magenta
- High-contrast single dramatic light source
- Dark fill, no light backgrounds ever

**Mood descriptor for prompts:** "Confident, cinematic, visually urgent — like a news graphic from 2035. Tense and transactional where appropriate. Professional but never corporate."

**What to avoid:**
- White or light backgrounds
- Generic stock photography feel
- Sans-serif clean/minimal corporate style
- Pastel or muted colors
- Any visual that could belong to a generic SaaS company

## Aspect Ratios & Crop Rules

| Format | Dimensions | Primary Use |
|---|---|---|
| 16:9 Landscape | 1920×1080 (or 3840×2160) | YouTube thumbnails, LinkedIn, primary episode art |
| 1:1 Square | 1080×1080 | Instagram, podcast platforms, RSS |

**1:1 crop rule:** Always compose 16:9 images with the primary subject and all text fields center-frame so the center square crop remains fully readable and visually complete without recomposing. Verify all three text fields (headline, subheadline, CTA) remain visible in the crop before finalizing.

## Slide Deck Specific

**Background:** Pure black #000000
