## ðŸ“ˆ Research Summary Highlights

### ATS Best Practices
- Use reverse chronological format
- Standard fonts only (Arial, Calibri, Helvetica, Times New Roman)
- No images, graphics, tables, or text boxes
- Standard section headings ("Work Experience," "Education," "Skills")
- Keywords from job description integrated naturally
- 1-page ideal (2 pages acceptable for senior roles)

### STAR Method Achievement Bullets
**Format Options:**
1. Action-Result: "Implemented X, resulting in Y% improvement"
2. Result-Action: "Increased revenue by X% by implementing Y strategy"
3. Compact: "Achieved X result by taking Y action"

**Key Metrics:**
- Financial: Revenue, cost savings, ROI
- Performance: Time saved, efficiency gains, quality improvements
- Scale: Users served, team size, projects completed
- Comparison: Before/after, target/actual, you vs. average

### Cover Letter Formats
1. **Problem-Solution** (89% recruiter preference)
2. **Achievement-Focused** (best for technical roles)
3. **Narrative** (best for creative/mission-driven roles)
